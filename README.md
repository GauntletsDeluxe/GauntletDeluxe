# Gauntlets Deluxe

**Community-run, fan-made Custom Gauntlets for Geometry Dash!**

---

### About

**Gauntlets Deluxe** adds <cg>custom gauntlets</c> to the game picked with <cl>support with the community</c>. This mod contains <co>fan-made gauntlets</c> based on its theme.

##### <cy>You can find a new cool button on the official gauntlets menu at the top right corner!</c>

---

### Features

- <cp>**Levels Points**</c>: Earned by completing levels in a gauntlet.
- <cr>**Gauntlet Points**</c>: Earn points by completing any of these fan-made gauntlets. Gauntlet points are based on the amount of levels and their difficulty.
- <cy>**Gauntlet Leaderboard**</c>: Compete with other users for the top spot on the leaderboard!
- <cf>**Fan-Made Creator Contest Gauntlets**</c>: Participate in any upcoming Gauntlet Creator Contests!
- <cb>**Entirely Community-run**</c>: This mod is entirely run by the community with <cl>**only little gauntlet moderation**</c> to keep it controlled, from the development of the gauntlets to the management of the leaderboard.

---

### <cp>Level Points</c>

#### Level Points are measured in the following criteria:

- **Auto - Easy**: 1 <cp>Level Points</c>
- **Normal**: 2 <cp>Level Points</c>
- **Hard** _(4-5 stars)_: 3 <cp>Level Points</c>
- **Harder** _(6-7 stars)_ - **Insane** _(8-9 stars)_: 4 <cp>Level Points</c>
- **Easy-Medium Demon**: 5 <cp>Level Points</c>
- **Hard-Extreme Demon**: 7 <cp>Level Points</c>

<cy>*If you have already beaten the level before using this mod, you still need to rebeat them to earn Level Points.*</c>

---

### <cr>Gauntlet Points</c>

#### Gauntlet Points are measured in the following criteria:

- **Auto - Hard Level Mix**: 3 <cr>Gauntlet Points</c>
- **Hard - Insane Level Mix**: 5 <cr>Gauntlet Points</c>
- **Easy Demon - Medium Demon Mix**: 10 <cr>Gauntlet Points</c>
- **Medium - Extreme Demon Mix**: 15 <cr>Gauntlet Points</c>
